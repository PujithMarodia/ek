Magnesium

Magnesium is a chemical element with symbol Mg and atomic number 12. It is a shiny gray-white lightweight metal, two-thirds the density of aluminium. Magnesium is the third-most-commonly-used structural metal, following iron and aluminium and is used in super-strong, lightweight materials and alloys.

Symbol: Mg

Atomic number: 12

Atomic mass: 24.305 u

Element category: Alkaline earth metal

Group: group 2

Fun Facts!

1. Magnesium is named after the Greek city of Magnesia.

2. Magnesium is the ninth most abundant element in the universe.

3. Magnesium forms in large stars as a result of fusion of helium with neon.
